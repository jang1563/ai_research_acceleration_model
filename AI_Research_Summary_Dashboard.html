Key Insights

1. Shift Type Determines Outcome

Type III (capability) shifts achieve 1.6-24x; Type II (efficiency) achieve 2.1-2.3x; Type I (scale) creates backlogs without throughput gains.

2. Drug Discovery Ceiling

Clinical trials remain 5-7 years regardless of AI. Three case studies (Recursion, Isomorphic, Insilico) all converge on 1.6-2.5x end-to-end.

3. Lab Automation is the Key Unlock

Lab automation unlocks 10-15x improvement over AI-only scenarios. Without it, cognitive gains create backlogs rather than throughput.

4. Model Validated

Mean validation score of 0.74 across 9 studies. Type II shifts best predicted (0.80-0.97); Type III breakthroughs require expanded M_max.
